B2C E-Commerce, Shopify

EineWeltGF is a niche e-commerce platform dedicated to gluten-free products, designed to serve health-conscious consumers with high-quality, allergen-free options. This freelance project was developed entirely by me—from initial client consultation to final deployment—using the Shopify framework to ensure scalability, performance, and ease of management for the client.

  • CLIENTEine Welt Glutenfrei
  • YEAR2025
  • CATEGORYE-Commerce, Shopify Development
  • LIVE PROJECTVisit site

RESEARCH

Before development, I collaborated closely with the client to understand their product range, target audience, and operational needs. I analyzed competitor stores, user behavior in the gluten-free market, and Shopify capabilities to craft a solution that balanced clean design, smooth UX, and simple backend workflows.

DESIGN

I designed a modern, clean, and user-friendly interface tailored to health-conscious shoppers. The visual identity reflects trust and clarity, with intuitive navigation, product filtering, and a mobile-first approach. The overall design ensures that users can easily discover products and complete purchases with minimal friction.

DEVELOPMENT

Built on the Shopify platform, I handled the entire development cycle of the e-commerce shop—from theme customization to frontend design and backend configuration. I communicated directly with the client to gather and refine requirements, ensuring the final product aligned with their vision and business goals. My responsibilities included designing page layouts, collections, and product templates, customizing Shopify themes to reflect the brand identity, setting up essential apps and secure payment systems, and managing SEO-friendly URLs and metadata. The final result was a fully functional, visually cohesive, and performance-optimized online store, ready to attract and convert gluten-free product customers.

CONCEPT

The goal was to create an e-commerce store that not only reflected the client’s values—health, trust, and quality—but also empowered them with a reliable and easy-to-manage platform. This project showcases my ability to independently lead full-cycle Shopify projects, combining technical development with direct client communication and thoughtful design execution.
